Woolamai Productions: Classic Style Guide

A guide to the visual and tonal identity of Woolamai Productions, combining the tranquility of Cape Woolamai with the dynamism of modern content creation.

1. Brand Overview: The Essence

Woolamai Productions is a personal content creation company rooted in family, history, and heritage. Specializing in website development, audiobooks, and fun videos, our focus is on bringing cherished family situations, albums, and stories to life. While primarily serving our family, the visual identity is crafted with broader applications in mind, exuding a unique blend of classic elegance and modern playfulness.

Our core spirit is the harmonious convergence of different creative streams – video, photo, music, and audio all coming together – much like a beautiful symphony or a perfectly composed visual.

4. Typography

Our typography blends classic elegance with modern clarity, allowing for both sophisticated storytelling and playful expressiveness. We now define specific usage for UI elements.

Primary Heading Font (Serif)

Playfair Display

Used for major headlines, titles, and elements where a strong, classic, and sophisticated presence is desired. Its elegant curves and high contrast create a luxurious feel.

Usage: Page titles, section headings (H1, H2)

Secondary Heading / Modern Accent Font (Sans-Serif)

Montserrat

Ideal for subheadings, navigation, short statements, and instances requiring a clean, modern, and sharp aesthetic. Its geometric forms provide excellent readability and a contemporary edge.

Usage: Sub-headings (H3, H4), button text, navigation links, card titles, form labels, table headers.

Body Text Font (Serif)

Lora

Designed for long-form content, such as audiobooks, historical narratives, and detailed website text. Lora offers a traditional, highly legible, and sophisticated reading experience, making it perfect for conveying heritage and depth.

Usage: All body paragraphs, detailed descriptions, table cell content, input field text.

Playful / Expressive Usage

Fun Moments!

Playfulness can be introduced not necessarily with a separate "fun" font, but by using the existing fonts (like Montserrat) in bold, uppercase, with increased letter-spacing, or by varying sizes and weights dramatically. This maintains overall brand cohesion while allowing for joyful expression where appropriate (e.g., video titles, call-outs, prominent features).

5. Imagery & Photography Style

Our visual content will span a vast timeline, from 150-year-old historical photos to modern, fun, and Instagrammable moments. The key is to create a cohesive yet diverse visual narrative.

  • Historical Images: Will be treated with respect, potentially using subtle sepia tones, desaturation, or a consistent soft vignette to evoke a sense of age and nostalgia. Framing with minimalist borders can add a "museum exhibit" quality.
  • Modern & Fun Imagery: Will embrace vibrancy and authenticity. A consistent light and color grade (perhaps slightly warm to tie into the orange, or cool to tie into the teal, depending on context) will be applied to maintain brand consistency. Emphasis on candid, joyful moments.
  • Unification Techniques: Consider using consistent aspect ratios, minimalist overlays (e.g., a transparent hint of our teal or orange), or strategic cropping to unify the diverse collection. The focus should always be on storytelling, making each image a window into a part of our family's narrative.
  • Video & Audiovisual: Visuals for videos and audio content will reflect the dynamic nature of our production, using cuts, transitions, and motion graphics that feel both sharp and sophisticated, while incorporating the brand colors and typography.
  • No Images: No images (from URLs or base64) are included in this style guide. The descriptions and SVG are provided to illustrate concepts only.

7. Tone of Voice

The voice of Woolamai Productions is a reflection of its unique identity: personal, professional, and deeply rooted in connection.

  • Warm & Engaging: Approachable and inviting, especially when sharing family stories and heritage.
  • Knowledgeable & Clear: When discussing history, processes (like website development), or providing information.
  • Joyful & Approachable: For lighter content, celebrating milestones, and encouraging participation.
  • Respectful & Appreciative: Honoring the past, the people, and the stories we share.
  • Authentic: True to who we are, without pretension, focusing on genuine experiences.
